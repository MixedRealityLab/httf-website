SIGCHI Student Rate

Thanks to sponsorship from ACM SIGCHI, we are able to offer 80 subsidised student places at the reduced rate of £60. Our registration price for students without the discount is £160.

To receive this rate, ACM stipulates that you must have both ACM and ACM SIGCHI memberships ($19 each) at the time of registration. You must sign up from the ACM SIGCHI website on this page. You do not need to register for ACM SIGCHI membership until we contact you.

Registration discount codes

The discount is administered through the form of discount codes being issued to those who apply. These are then used during online registration to unlock the discounted rate.

You should request a registration discount code using this online form. This form will stay open even after we have exceeded 80 allocated discount codes, so that we can reallocate discount codes given to students who later decide not to attend. We will let you know if you are on the waiting list and will inform you right away if and when a code becomes available.

We will send the discount codes to the people who have requested a code, giving priority to those who have had a paper accepted. Remaining discount codes will be given on a first-come, first-served basis to those who have submitted, but were not accepted. After this, any remaining codes will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis to those interested in attending.

You will be emailed instructions on how to complete registration within two weeks of the discount code being sent to you.

Pre-apply to receive the discount

Please pre-apply to receive the discount. We will start issuing codes once decisions have been made on papers.

You do not need to be a member of ACM SIGCHI until completing your online registration.

If you have any questions, please contact our Sponsorship Chair.
