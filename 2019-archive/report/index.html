Report a concern

If you wish to report a concern regarding someone's behaviour at, or in relation to Halfway to the Future, you may do so using the form below. This form sends an email to Martin Porcheron (Communications Chair) and Pepita Stringer (Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion Chair). Alternatively, you may send a personal email to any/all of the General Chairs or the committee, or during the symposium you may approach us in person.

You may optionally include your name and email address in this report and can choose whether or not you wish for us to contact you regarding your report. If you wish to remain anonymous, please enter a pseudonym (this can even be a random string of characters!).

Careline and WhatsApp

During the symposium, attendees will be able to contact Martin Porcheron (Communications Chair and Treasurer) using the UK mobile phone number ‭+44 7394 212757‬ at any time either by calling, sending an SMS, or sending a WhatsApp message.
